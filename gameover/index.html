WELCOME TO VERSUS

Big Night Entertainment Group is proud to introduce Boston’s first arcade bar in Downtown Crossing. VERSUS is a new concept from the BNEG team which masterfully combines retro-modern arcade games with iconic pop art murals from local artist Dirty Dek in a thematic and high-energy bar setting. The space boasts LED televisions, a Funktion One sound system, and design elements inspired by 80’s pop culture. VERSUS is open daily for lunch and dinner, serving pub grub, creative and fun cocktails, as well as craft beer and wine, and is also available for private events.
